# CCPA/CPRA Compliance Framework

## Overview

The California Consumer Privacy Act (as amended by CPRA) applies to the Quant platform as it may process personal information of California residents.

## Consumer Rights

### Right to Know (Section 1798.100)

- **Implementation**: Privacy dashboard showing collected data categories
- **Response Time**: Within 45 days (extendable by 45 days with notice)
- **Verification**: Multi-step identity verification before disclosure
- **Lookback Period**: 12 months of data collection history

### Right to Delete (Section 1798.105)

- **Implementation**: Account deletion with cascade to service providers
- **Exceptions**: Legal obligations, internal security, bug detection
- **Response Time**: Within 45 days
- **Service Providers**: Directed to delete within same timeframe

### Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing (Section 1798.120)

- **Implementation**: "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" link
- **Scope**: No sale of personal information; sharing limited to service delivery
- **GPC Signal**: Global Privacy Control browser signal honored

### Right to Correct (Section 1798.106)

- **Implementation**: Self-service profile editing
- **Response Time**: Within 45 days
- **Verification**: Identity verification before corrections applied

### Right to Limit Use of Sensitive PI (Section 1798.121)

- **Implementation**: Sensitive data processing limited to service delivery
- **Categories**: Login credentials, precise geolocation, communications

## Non-Discrimination (Section 1798.125)

- No price differences for exercising privacy rights
- No service-level degradation for privacy choices
- Financial incentive programs (if any) require opt-in consent

## Financial Incentives

- Any loyalty or rewards programs clearly disclose data value exchange
- Opt-in consent required before enrollment
- Easy withdrawal mechanism provided

## Privacy Notice Requirements

### At Collection Notice

- Categories of personal information collected
- Purposes for each category
- Retention periods per category
- Link to full privacy policy

### Categories Collected

| Category          | Examples             | Purpose              | Retention                  |
| ----------------- | -------------------- | -------------------- | -------------------------- |
| Identifiers       | Email, name, user ID | Account management   | Account lifetime + 30 days |
| Commercial Info   | Subscription history | Billing              | 7 years (legal)            |
| Internet Activity | Log data, usage      | Service improvement  | 90 days                    |
| Geolocation       | IP-based location    | Security, compliance | 30 days                    |
| Professional Info | Organization name    | Account features     | Account lifetime           |

## Service Provider Requirements

- Written contracts with all service providers
- Restrictions on use beyond contracted services
- Certification of compliance with CCPA obligations
- Annual assessment of service provider practices

## Metrics and Reporting

- Annual disclosure of request statistics
- Categories: requests received, complied with, denied
- Median response time tracked and reported
